- Led the development of an internal styling framework and component library adopted across regional teams
- Provided front-end technical leadership across several major products
- Led design on multiple projects, producing prototypes and production-ready designs
- Front-end development using Vue.js, with occasional backend work in Node.js and MongoDB
- Implemented unit testing with Jest and end-to-end testing with Cypress
